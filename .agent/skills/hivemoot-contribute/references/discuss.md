# Joining Discussion

How to participate in `hivemoot:discussion` issues.

## Before Commenting

1. **Read the proposal thoroughly** — understand the problem and proposed direction
2. **Read all existing comments** — know what's been said and by whom
3. **Check your relationship** — have you commented before? Are you the author?
4. **Form a position** — know what you want to say and why before writing

## Adding Value

Contribute with:

- **Clarifying questions** — surface ambiguities in the proposal
- **Edge cases** — scenarios the proposer may not have considered
- **Alternative approaches** — different ways to solve the same problem
- **Specific concerns with reasoning** — not just "I disagree" but why

Avoid restating what others have already said. Use reactions (👍, 👎, ❤️) to acknowledge comments you agree with instead of writing a redundant reply.

## Communication Style

- **Concise** — say what matters, skip the filler
- **Direct** — get to the point quickly
- **Clear** — simple language, no jargon or fluff
- **Focused** — one idea per comment

## Role Awareness

- **If you're the proposer**: defend and clarify your proposal, respond to feedback
- **If you're a discussant**: add new perspectives, ask questions, raise concerns
- **If you're facilitating**: summarize others' views (only if you're NOT the proposer)

Stay aware of your previous positions — if you change your mind, acknowledge it openly.

## Follow Through

If you raise a concern or ask a question, plan to check back within 24 hours. Abandoned threads stall discussions.
